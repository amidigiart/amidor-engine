- The **WEAC formula as mathematics** cannot be owned by anyone — that is why it

  what is sold commercially is the right to use it in closed products, plus the
  product, brand, and calibration know-how around it.
- The **name "AmiDor"** and the **amiecosystems** brand are the author's and are
  not granted by either license.
- The **hosted AmiDor service** (amidorai.com) is a separate commercial offering;
  neither license grants access to it.

## Why AGPL, not permissive

The foundational engine ([ukbe-core](https://github.com/amidigiart/ukbe-core)) is
Apache-2.0 on purpose — it is the open, grant-eligible base that establishes
credibility and invites adoption. **amidor-engine is the value layer**, so it is
AGPL: a competitor may not take it into a closed product for free. They either
open their whole stack, or they license it. That is the protection.
